Dots and Boxes

Rules:

Players take turns joining two horizontally or vertically adjacent dots by a line. A player that completes the fourth side of a square, and makes a box, clicks the center of the box and indicates if they are "player 1" or "player 2". Then that same player must play again. When all the boxes have been colored, press the button that reads "Click here when the game is over". The player who has more colored boxes wins. Player 1 is red and player 2 is blue. To reset the game, refresh the page.
